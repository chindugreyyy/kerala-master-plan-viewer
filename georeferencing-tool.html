Welcome to the Georeferencing Tool

  1. Select a city from the dropdown above
  2. Click "Load Map" to display the extracted map page
  3. Click on the map image to place a control point
  4. Enter the real-world coordinates (latitude/longitude) for that point
  5. Add at least 10-15 points spread across the entire map
  6. Click "Export GCP CSV" to save the control points
  7. Use the exported CSV with the georeferencing pipeline

Tip: Use Google Maps or OpenStreetMap to find accurate coordinates for landmarks.

Good landmarks: Railway stations, temples, bridges, major intersections, government buildings
